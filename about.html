About Us

Building a Bridge Between

Our Mission

Making Public Services Work for You

We believe that every citizen deserves responsive public services. NagrikSetu was created to eliminate the frustration of unanswered complaints by providing a structured, trackable, and transparent resolution process.

From potholes to water supply issues, our platform ensures your voice reaches the right authority — and that action is taken.

The principles that guide everything we build and every complaint we help resolve.

Transparency

Every complaint is tracked openly. Citizens can monitor progress.

Accountability

Trust & Security
