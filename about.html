About NagrikSetu

Bridging the gap between citizens and authorities through transparency and technology.

Our Mission

NagrikSetu is designed to empower citizens by giving them a simple platform to raise complaints and ensure that their voices are heard. Our mission is to create a transparent system where issues are tracked, monitored, and resolved efficiently.

What We Do

We provide a digital interface where users can submit complaints related to public services such as roads, electricity, water, and sanitation. Each complaint is routed to the appropriate authority and tracked in real-time.

Why It Matters

Many issues in communities go unresolved due to lack of proper communication. NagrikSetu aims to eliminate this gap by providing accountability, transparency, and ease of access for every citizen.
